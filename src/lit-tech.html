Context

Lit Technologies was an M-commerce marketplace established in December 2017, serving kirana stores and HoReCa clients in Trivandrum, Kerala. The model was simple but operationally complex — store owners placed orders through the app before 7pm, and goods were delivered the next working day.

As Head of Design, I led the overall development of the brand, platform, customer support, and post-sales experience. The role was roughly split: 40% product design (UI/UX), 40% sales and brand management, and 20% customer relations — essentially building every customer-facing touchpoint from the ground up.

B2B Consumer Ordering App

Designed and iterated the B2B ordering app for kirana stores. The user base was predominantly local language speakers (70%+), which shaped every design decision — from simplified navigation to vernacular-first communication.

The app grew from 89 installs at launch (April 2018) to 5,000+ installs with 1,900+ monthly active orderers by December 2020, through continuous iteration based on user interviews and in-field testing.

Internal Delivery & Routing App

Designed the internal operations suite — a delivery management app used by drivers to execute daily routes, manage store-level deliveries, capture cash reconciliation, and handle returns. The system tracked over 12 million kgs of goods delivered in under 28 months.

Acquisition & Migration to Udaan

In October 2020, Lit Technologies was acquired by Udaan — India's largest B2B trade platform. Post-acquisition, led the platform migration achieving 100% user retention, launched Udaan in Kochi within three months, and onboarded major FMCG brands including HUL, Coke, Nestle, P&G, PepsiCo, and Reckitt.

The acquisition validated the platform's approach — the simplified app workflow designed for local language speakers at Lit Technologies directly informed adaptations made to the Udaan platform for the Kerala market.
