Case Study · Oct 2022 – Oct 2025

ONDC: Building India's Digital
Commerce Infrastructure

Among the first 20 employees at ONDC — India's DPIIT-backed national commerce protocol. Led digital programs and social-sector category growth, working across central ministries, state bodies, foundations, and network participants.

Role Evolution

Joined ONDC when the team was under 20 people, initially leading Web Engagement and Programs Management reporting to the CBO. Later promoted to Lead Category Growth — Social Sector (SGI), reporting directly to the CEO.

The role spanned two distinct phases: first, building the foundational digital infrastructure and communications toolkit that the entire network would rely on; second, leveraging that infrastructure to drive social impact and economic inclusion at national scale.

Phase 2: Social Impact & Government Partnerships

🤝 Ministry Partnerships

Operationalised partnerships with the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) and Ministry of Textiles (MoT), enabling artisans and Self-Help Groups to achieve their first-ever digital sales on a national platform. Activated listings across 15+ state and central emporiums including Central Cottage Industries Emporium, TRIFED (Tribes India), IndiaHandmade, and eSaras.

Presented ONDC to all State Rural Livelihood Missions (SRLMs) for adoption across states. Coordinated with AP-MEPMA and SERP for rural e-commerce rollout in Andhra Pradesh.

📈 Social Sector Growth

Architected and executed targeted growth sprints delivering sustained month-over-month GMV growth in the social impact sector for six consecutive months. Authored the Supply Playbook addressing operational inefficiencies for rural and first-time sellers — reversing sector de-growth by systematically improving catalogue quality, fulfilment rates, and seller readiness.

✅ Verifiable Credentials (BMGF-funded)

Supported the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation-funded initiative to enhance seller trust and verification on the network. Increased adoption of verifiable credentials (GI Tag, Women-Owned, Organic) and scaled credentialed sellers significantly.

🏆 AP Women's Day Campaign — Guinness World Record

Facilitated the Andhra Pradesh Women's Day campaign in collaboration with state government bodies, generating 3 lakh orders worth ₹5.13 crore in just one week for women entrepreneurs. The campaign set an official Guinness World Record for most users in an e-business video lesson.

Communications & Network Expansion

Created the first ONDC adaptable communications — Story Books, Onboarding Flyers — for network participants, sellers, and government ministries to explain the complex concept of an open network. Led comms for high-profile events including Startup Mahakumbh, G20 Summit, and Bharat Tex.

Spearheaded the launch of the Media & Entertainment domain on the ONDC network, creating a strategic integration pipeline of 13+ organisations including HT Media, Times Network, and Nazara.
